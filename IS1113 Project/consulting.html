Hyperautomation

< p>Hyperautomation is the combination of multiple machine learning, packaged software and and automation tools to deliver work, and deals with the application of advanced technologies, including artifical intelligence and machine learning, to increasingly automate processes and augment humans. Hyperautomation extends across a range of tools that can be automated. Hyperautomation brings together several components of process automation, integrating tools and technologies that amplify the ability to automate work.The idea is to automate more and more knowledge work,and engage everyone in an organisation to be part of the transformation.


Multiexperience

The term multiexperience incorporates all the ways users interact with a brand, and the multitude of physical devices deivering the user experiences resulting from those interactions. Multexperience development involves creating fit-for-purpose apps, while simultaneously ensuring a consistent user experience.A multiexperience development platform enables you to develop one app and then deploy it across a whole variety of devices and platforms.
