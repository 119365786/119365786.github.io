Sports

From a young age I have always had an interest in sports. My favourite sport is swimming; although ironically I only learned how to swim after I nearly drowned on a family holiday. After this incident, I was enrolled in swimming lessons, and ever since then I have progressed steadily and my love of the sport has grown. A few years later I joined my local swimming club. When I reached the competitive squad in the club, I began competing in swimming galas around the country. I enrolled in water safety classes at the pool, and completed all the levels required to become a pool lifeguard. The summer after I completed my water safety levels, I joined the Cork Surf Lifesaving Club. During the summer this club trained twice a week on youghal beach, where we practiced skills like paddling on surfboards, and using rescue tubes and skiis. Swimming is a passion of mine, and encouraged me to get involved in water safety. I am now a qualified lifeguard, and work part time in the pool where I learned how to swim.

Travel

Travelling is something I place a lot of value on. I think it is very important to see various parts of the world, so we can broaden our horizons, and increase our knowledge of different cultures. Unfortunately, I have not had the opportunuty to do a huge amount of travelling just yet. However I have visited Wales, England, and Portugal. When I finish college I plan to travel for a while before I find employment.
